As you progress through levels, enemies become stronger.
Every 10th level is a boss level. Bosses have 500 HP and deal 50 damage.
After level 5, a heart has a 2% chance to spawn each second.
Hearts that spawn randomly after level 5 will fully heal you!
As you progress in levels, your firerate increases, allowing you to shoot faster.
Crystals have a 10% chance of spawning every three seconds.
Crystals will give you 300 extra points to your score!
Please avoid any and all projectiles...
